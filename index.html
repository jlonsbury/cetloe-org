🗐 Make a copy

Download a complete, self-contained file you can send to anyone — they just double-click to open it. Pick which version it shows and which saved versions to include.

Set a password and the copy's contents are encrypted — viewers must enter it to see the chart, so it's safe to post on a public web page. (Make protected copies in Chrome, Edge, or Safari.)

🗂 Teams & Divisions

Rename divisions and teams in bulk, or add new ones — a rename applies to everyone in that division or team. Pick a division's color with its swatch, and give a team an optional description. Divisions are set on the org chart, not here: open a person's card and tick “heads their division,” and everyone reporting under them inherits it. Member counts update automatically. To move one person, edit their card instead.

🏷 Tags

Create tags to group people any way you like — e.g. “Reclass 2026”, “Search committee”, “Remote”. Add tags to people from their card (Tags section), filter by tag in the Staff Database, and highlight a group in the org chart. Member counts update automatically.
